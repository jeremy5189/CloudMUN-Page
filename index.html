No more Powerpoints and Flashdrives

As a chair/co-chair at a MUN conference, you hate to exchange conference powerpoints with your dais team. CloudMUN store all your conference and data on the cloud. All you need is login.

Highlights

  • Realtime Syncing

    Datas and synced realtime to your Dais team.

  • Majority Calculation

    We know you are lazy, so do we.

  • Timer integration

    No more iPhone timer, everything you need in one interface.

  • Conference Timeline

    Record everything which occured in the conference room. Remotely mointor every conference realtime.

  • Autocomplete

    Save time and to avoid typo during sessions.

  • Offline support

    Accident happens. But we are here to prevent it.

ROP Support

The following Rules of Procedure are currently supported by CloudMUN.

  • Roll Call
  • Setting Agenda
  • General Speakers' List
  • Secondary Speakers' List
  • Moderated Caucus
  • Unmoderated Caucus
  • Post./Resum./Closure of Debate
  • Introduction of DR
  • Friendly/Unfriendly Amendment
  • Suspension/Adjour. of Meeting
  • Reorder DR
  • Division of Question
  • Roll Call Vote
  • Simple Majority
  • Vote by Acclamation
